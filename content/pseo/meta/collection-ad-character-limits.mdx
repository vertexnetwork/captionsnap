Meta Collection Ads pair a hero creative (image or video) with a 4-tile product grid below it. Hero copy uses Feed character limits — 125 visible primary text, 40 visible headline. Each product tile in the grid caps at 30 characters of title before truncation.

The format is designed for catalog-style merchandising. Tap the hero, the user opens an Instant Experience full-screen view; tap a product tile, the user goes to that product's page. Both navigation paths have their own copy budgets.

The 30-character product title clamp is the biggest catalog-feed surprise. Most product catalogs export titles like "Ultra-Soft Cotton Crewneck T-Shirt — Size M, Heather Gray" which clamp brutally to "Ultra-Soft Cotton Crewneck T-S…" in the tile. Plan a catalog-feed override for Collection Ads that uses 30-character titles, not the full marketing title.

## The catalog-feed override

For Collection Ads, maintain a separate `display_title` field in your catalog feed that fits in 30 characters. Use that for the tile, and reserve the full marketing title for the product detail page.
