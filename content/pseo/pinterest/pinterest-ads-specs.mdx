This is the complete 2026 Pinterest Ads character spec across all four ad formats. Pinterest's spec is unusual: every format shares the **same text limits** — a 100-character title (~40 visible in the mobile feed) and an 800-character description (~50 visible) — and differs only in media and which UI overlays sit on top. Idea Ads are the one exception: their per-page text overlay follows its own rule. The recurring trap is that Pinterest's upload form and Help Center quote the *maximum* (100 / 800) while the *visible* window in-feed is far smaller, and that gap is undocumented.

## Standard Pin Ad

Single 2:3 image. The default format and the highest-volume one.

## Carousel Ad

2–5 cards, each with its own 100-character title and 800-character description, same ~40/~50 visible windows per card.

## Idea Ad

The exception. Multi-page (3–20 pages); the per-page text overlay caps at 250 characters with ~100 visible — a different rule from the title field.

## What carries across every format

- **Title: 100 max, ~40 visible in mobile feed.** Lead with the search keyword; finish the thought by character 40.
- **Description: 800 max, ~50 visible in mobile feed (~200 desktop).** Primarily a search-matching field, not in-feed persuasion.
- **The image carries the sell.** Pinterest users are in save-for-later search mode; text routes intent, the visual converts.
- **Aspect ratio: 2:3 vertical (1000×1500).** Other ratios render letterboxed and lose feed real estate.
