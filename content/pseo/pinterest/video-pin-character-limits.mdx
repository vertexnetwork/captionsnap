Pinterest Video Pin Ads share the same shell as Standard Pin Ads — a 2:3 vertical layout with title and description below — but replace the static image with autoplay video that runs muted on first impression. Title still allows 100 chars (~40 visible on mobile), description still allows 800 chars (~50 visible mobile, ~200 desktop). Video Pin runs in the same feed contexts as Standard Pin (Home feed, search results, related-pin rail) and uses the same SEO indexing.

The biggest mistake on Pinterest Video Pin is treating it like TikTok or Reels. Pinterest viewers do not stop scrolling for video — they save it. The video format works as a second-stage closer for users who already saved the pin and tap into the detail view. Caption your video creative for sound-off comprehension, but invest the production in the still-frame thumbnail, since that is what drives the save behavior.
