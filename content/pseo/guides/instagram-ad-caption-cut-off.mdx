An Instagram ad caption that reads in full inside Ads Manager but shows **"… more"** in the live feed isn't a bug — Instagram clips the caption at a tighter window than Facebook, and the preview tool doesn't simulate it. If your value proposition or CTA sits after that "… more", most of your paid audience never sees it.

## The Instagram-specific cut

Instagram Feed collapses the caption to roughly the first **125 characters** before "… more", but the practical hook window is tighter than Facebook because the image dominates more screen height before the caption begins. On Reels and Stories the text is overlaid on creative and the safe-zone furniture (profile, CTA, audio tag) eats the edges entirely.

## How to fix it

1. **Write the first line as a standalone ad.** It should make the case with zero expansion.
2. **Never split a sentence across the cut.** A caption that clips mid-thought reads as broken and depresses tap-through.
3. **Verify the live render before launch** — paste the exact caption into the simulator and read only what shows pre-"more".
