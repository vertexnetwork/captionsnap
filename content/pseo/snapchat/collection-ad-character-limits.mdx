Snapchat Collection Ads pair a hero snap (image or video) with a 4-product tile strip rendered along the bottom of the screen. The hero inherits Single Image / Video limits — 25-character brand name, 34-character headline, both fully visible. The product tiles below pull from a connected catalog and don't expose customizable text directly; product names render only after a user taps a tile.

The format is purpose-built for direct-response commerce: the hero snap creates aspiration, the tile strip lets the user tap straight into product detail without leaving the snap. The 4-tile constraint is hard — Collection cannot show 5 or more products at once. Catalog feeds with more than 4 products rotate the visible tiles per impression based on user signals.

## Catalog vs static tiles

Collection Ads support two tile modes: catalog-driven (tiles auto-pull from a connected product feed) or static (4 manually-uploaded tiles). Catalog mode delivers higher click-through but requires a maintained feed. Static mode is faster to ship for one-off promotions.
