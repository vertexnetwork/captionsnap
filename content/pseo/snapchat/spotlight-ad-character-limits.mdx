Snapchat Spotlight is the platform's TikTok-style vertical short-form video feed. Spotlight Ads run inline with organic Spotlight content using the same UI vocabulary: a full-bleed 9:16 video, right-rail engagement (like / comment / share), username block bottom-left, and a sponsored CTA strip. Display name caps at 25 characters with full visibility. Caption text clips at roughly 60 visible characters before "...more" — similar to YouTube Shorts, tighter than TikTok In-Feed.

Spotlight ads inherit Snapchat's broader audience profile (younger, mobile-native, ad-aware) but with the engagement model of TikTok. Plan creative for sound-on auto-play, captions still required for accessibility, and a tight 60-character caption that lands the hook before the "...more" tap.

## Why 60 not 100

Snapchat Spotlight's right-rail engagement column is identical to TikTok's footprint, but Snapchat layers an additional brand chip on top of the username row, which compresses the visible caption window from ~100 down to ~60. Plan accordingly.
