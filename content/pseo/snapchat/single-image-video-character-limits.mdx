Snapchat Single Image / Video Ads (often just called "Snap Ads") have the tightest text limits on any major social platform. Brand name caps at 25 characters and is fully visible — no truncation. Headline caps at 34 characters and is also fully visible. There is no description, body text, or expandable caption. The CTA itself is selected from a Snapchat-defined preset list ("Shop Now," "Sign Up," "Watch," etc.) and cannot be customized.

The strategic implication is that Snapchat's text fields function as labels, not copy. The visual tells the story; the headline punctuates it. "Buy the new collection" beats "Discover the curated drop that changes how you dress for spring." Snapchat's audience skews younger and more visually-driven than Meta or LinkedIn, and the format reinforces that.
