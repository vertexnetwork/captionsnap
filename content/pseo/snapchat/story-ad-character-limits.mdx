Snapchat Story Ads run as a multi-snap collection (3 to 20 snaps) that lives on the Discover surface — Snapchat's curated content tab. Users tap a tile on Discover to enter the story, and the brand's snaps play sequentially with skip-between-snaps available. Brand name on the tile is capped at 25 characters; tile headline at 34 characters. Each individual snap inside the story has its own text-overlay budget tied to creative design (no platform-enforced caption limit on the snap content itself, but text overlays are capped at 250 chars in the editor).

The Discover-tile chrome covers the top 14% of the screen with the tile's brand and headline rendered over the cover snap. Plan creative with the central 72% as the safe canvas, mirroring the Stories pattern from Instagram and Facebook.

## Snap count optimization

Story Ads with 3-5 snaps have the highest completion rate. Story Ads with 10+ snaps lose roughly 15% completion per additional snap past 5. Plan tight; expand only if the narrative requires it.
