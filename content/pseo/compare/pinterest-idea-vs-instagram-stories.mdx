Pinterest Idea Ads and Instagram Stories Ads are both multi-page vertical formats — but they exist for different stages of the user funnel. Instagram Stories is mid-feed interruption, optimized for awareness and direct-response (swipe-up CTA on every snap). Pinterest Idea Ads are saveable storyboards, optimized for save-for-later behavior with a single end-of-flow CTA.

Character economics: Pinterest Idea allows 100 visible chars per page-overlay text + 40-char title. Instagram Stories allows 90 visible chars primary text + 30-char headline. Per-page Pinterest is slightly more generous; per-ad Instagram is slightly tighter.

The bigger structural difference: Pinterest Idea Ads scale up to 20 pages. Instagram Stories caps individual ads at typically 1-3 sequential snaps. Pinterest rewards storyboard depth; Stories rewards sharp single-frame impact.
