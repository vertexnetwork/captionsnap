Instagram Stories and TikTok Spark Ads are both vertical 9:16 placements but represent fundamentally different mental models for the viewer. Stories are sealed-slot, time-bounded, branded-feeling. Spark Ads are For You feed, infinite-scroll, organic-feeling. The character math reflects the difference.

Stories' primary text caps at 125 characters total with 90 visible. Spark Ads inherit In-Feed: 2,200 maximum, 100 visible. Stories' headline caps at 40 characters total. Spark Ads use a 40-character display name with no in-field truncation. Stories has no description field; Spark Ads doesn't use a description field either.

Safe zones differ meaningfully. Stories sacrifices the top 14% (progress + profile) and bottom 14% (sticker + CTA), leaving the middle 72%. Spark Ads sacrifices the right 14% (engagement column from 28%–82% Y) and the bottom 22% (username + caption + CTA strip), leaving an L-shaped safe area.

## When to choose which

Stories outperforms when the message is brand-focused, time-sensitive, and image-led. Spark Ads outperforms when the message is community-led, demonstration-heavy, and benefits from feeling organic.
