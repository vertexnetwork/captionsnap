X Amplify and YouTube Skippable In-Stream are both publisher-pre-roll formats, but the inventory and runtime models differ significantly.

X Amplify runs against a curated publisher list (NFL, MLB, ESPN, Bloomberg, etc.) for 6-15 seconds before partner video. Skippable Amplify enables skip after 5 seconds. The companion post is a standard 280-character X post.

YouTube Skippable In-Stream runs against any monetized YouTube content (4M+ creators) for up to 3 minutes. Skip enables after 5 seconds, but YouTube only bills on 30+ second views or click-through, so unwanted impressions are essentially free.

The trade-off: X Amplify offers brand-safe premium placement at a 3-5x CPM premium. YouTube Skippable offers massive scale at lower CPMs but with broader inventory variability.
