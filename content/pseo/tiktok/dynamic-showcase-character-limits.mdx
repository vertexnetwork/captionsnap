TikTok Dynamic Showcase Ads (DSA) auto-generate ad creative from a linked product catalog. The video creative is generated with TikTok's templating engine; the product card is pinned with title, price, and CTA. Caption inherits In-Feed limits (~100 visible characters of 2,200 max). Product titles in the pinned card truncate at 34 characters — same number as Shop Ads.

The catalog feed is where DSA campaigns succeed or fail. If your catalog `title` field is "Premium Organic Cotton T-Shirt — Heather Gray, Size M," it clamps to "Premium Organic Cotton T-Shirt — H…" in the pinned card. The fix is the same `display_title` pattern as Meta: maintain a 34-character version for the pinned-card surface.

Description in the pinned card shows the first 80 characters of your catalog `description` field. Plan a `display_description` of 80 characters or less.
