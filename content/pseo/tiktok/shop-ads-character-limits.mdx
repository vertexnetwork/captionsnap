TikTok Shop Ads pin a product card overlay onto an In-Feed video. The product card sits below the right rail and above the CTA strip, occupying the bottom 22% of vertical real estate (right of the username block). Inside that card, product titles truncate at 34 characters and product descriptions show the first 80 characters before clamping.

Caption space is technically unchanged from In-Feed (2,200-character maximum, 100-character visible window), but the product card cuts into the visual region where you'd usually compose hero copy. Practical caption budget on Shop Ads is closer to 80 visible characters once you account for the product card occluding the lower portion of the canvas.

The 34-character product title clamp is the most important number to memorize. Titles that work for organic listings (e.g. "Vintage Sterling Silver Earrings — Limited Edition Drop") get brutally cut on Shop Ads to "Vintage Sterling Silver Earrings…". Write Shop product titles to fit inside 34 characters or rewrite the catalog feed for the placement.
