TikTok Spark Ads boost an existing organic post into paid placement. They inherit the organic post's For You feed UI exactly — same right-rail engagement column, same username block, same caption clamp. The 2026 spec maps the right rail to the right 14% of the canvas from approximately 28% Y down to 82% Y, the username block to the bottom-left 70% × 8%, and the sponsored CTA strip to the bottom 8% across the full width.

Spark Ads outperform In-Feed for many advertisers precisely because they look organic. The downside: any creative that doesn't respect TikTok's safe zones looks broken in a way users associate with low-quality content. A Spark Ad with a clipped logo or covered CTA reads as careless, and TikTok audiences punish careless content with skip rate.

The 22% bottom band is double-occupied: the username + caption sit there alongside the sponsored CTA strip. If you compose copy or product shots into the bottom of your video, plan for both overlays simultaneously.
