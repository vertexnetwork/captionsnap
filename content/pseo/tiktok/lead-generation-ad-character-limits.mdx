TikTok Lead Generation Ads collect form fills inside the TikTok app — same mental model as Meta Lead Ads, different character math. The video creative inherits In-Feed clamps (2,200-character caption with ~100 visible, 40-character display name). When the user taps the CTA, an Instant Form opens with a 80-character form intro and configurable form fields.

The 80-character form intro is the field most Lead Generation campaigns waste. Buyers default to "Fill out this form to get more info" — generic, low-conversion. The pattern that performs is "What product are you considering? We'll send a sample." Specific, specific, specific.

Form fields support standard PII (name, email, phone) plus custom questions. Custom question labels cap at 90 characters — slightly tighter than Meta Lead Ads. The pre-fill behavior is identical: TikTok auto-fills from user profile data and the user can edit before submitting.
