TikTok Carousel Image Ads (sometimes called Photo Ads or Image Slideshows) place 2–35 images in a horizontal swipeable card stack, replacing the typical video creative. Caption inherits In-Feed clamps (2,200-character maximum, ~100 visible). Each card supports a 40-character per-card title that overlays the bottom of the image.

The 100-character caption clamp applies to the entire carousel, not per card — there's only one caption shared across all images. The per-card 40-character title is what differentiates each image. Use the caption for the global hook and the per-card titles for sequential narrative beats.

The format favors product-led ads with multiple SKUs or step-by-step explainers. Performance varies by category — fashion and home goods tend to outperform service categories on this format because the image-stack lends itself to product variety.

## Per-card narrative discipline

Plan each card's title as a beat in a story. Card 1 = hook. Card 2 = proof. Card 3 = offer. Card 4 = CTA reminder. Don't repeat the same title across cards.
