## Why YouTube Shorts is tighter than TikTok

YouTube's right-rail has six engagement icons (the "Remix" icon is unique to Shorts) versus TikTok's five, and the channel block on the bottom-left includes a "Subscribe" pill that TikTok's @username row doesn't have. The result is more pixels claimed by UI and a tighter description clamp.
