YouTube Masthead is reservation-only, sold by Google's direct-sales team for one-day or multi-day takeovers of the YouTube home page. The format runs autoplay video at the top of the home feed (muted by default, with a tap-to-unmute) and pairs it with a long headline (90 chars max, ~40 visible on mobile) and a 70-character description (~50 visible). On desktop the video and copy panel sit side-by-side; on mobile the copy stacks underneath.

Because Masthead is a fixed-price reservation product (typical day rates run six figures for major markets), the brief usually comes from a brand campaign launch rather than a performance-marketing ladder. The 40-character headline window is enough for a tagline, and the 50-character description carries the secondary message. There is no destination-page click — just a "Watch" button that pushes to the brand channel.
